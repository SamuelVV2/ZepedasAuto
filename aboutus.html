Welcome!

Uziel's Auto care is an auto repair shop based out of Chicago, IL. We have been in business for 15 years and have highly experienced technicians who bring years of knowledge, skill, and expertise. Whether you need car repair, auto body repair, or collision repair, we can help!

At Uziel's Auto care, we provide complete auto body repair, affordable service, and prompt response times. We know that if your car breaks down or you are in a collision, the last thing you want to stress about is how long the repairs will take. We technicians are driven and work to get your car back to you in tip-top shape as soon as possible. We work on all makes and models of cars and ensure you receive the highest quality repair work and customer service.

Uziel's Auto care provides routine maintenance and car services such as oil and filter changes and coolant flushes. We also work on suspensions and ensure that your vehicle not only rides smoothly, but that you have the ability to control your vehicle fully, especially when stopping and turning. We specialize in body work and will fix bumper scuffs and cracks, repair scratches and scrapes, and fill holes. Uziel's Auto care also offers dent removal and all repair related to collisions and accidents.
